Our Story

Born from a simple belief: mental well-being should feel human, accessible and connected to the world we live in.

MoodScale began with the idea that understanding emotions can help people, teams and communities live with more clarity and resilience.

For the EU initiative, we connect mental health, thoughtful technology, nature retreats, climate resilience and civil society partnerships.

The initiative

Building a happier world, one emotion at a time.

MoodScale EU is not a product showcase. It is an initiative to support mental well-being as part of a wider sustainability and climate-conscious future.

Inspired by the spirit of global climate conversations such as COP, we focus on the human side of sustainability: climate anxiety, emotional resilience, inclusion, community well-being and nature-connected healing.

01

Understand emotions

Self-awareness, assessments and emotional literacy to help people name what they feel without shame.

02

Support people

Therapist-led guidance, counseling pathways and care connections for individuals and families.

03

Strengthen communities

Workshops, workplace well-being and resilience programs for organisations and civil society.

04

Reconnect with nature

Nature retreats, digital detox and climate-aware well-being practices for healthier minds and planet.

Mental Health & Climate

We recognise the growing impact of climate anxiety and environmental stress on well-being. Through nature-based wellness experiences and resilience-building programs, we help people reconnect with themselves, their communities and the planet.

“When we heal people, we heal communities. When we heal communities, we heal the planet.”
